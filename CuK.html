Colt und Köcher

The difficult search for actors for the porn film ›Colt & Köcher

I was constantly trying to raise the standard, my next plan was to shoot a film outside. Uve Schmidt and I developed a scenario called 'Colt & Köcher', the message was that a couple of trappers were exploiting an Indian couple: 'Colt und Köcher oder Die Ausbeutung' or 'how the west was won'. An Indian couple is caught by the trapper couple. In this scenarion the exploitation equals sexual exploitation. As an Indian, we had Fatima Igrahim in mind, who at that time celebrated the happy early life in Frankfurt. With an estimated seventeen under influence of Bernd Malzahn from Hilden alias Brummbär and with the help of a waterbed, she had stylized herself as the original cell of all female liberation pornography. The simple dramaturgical means of the silent film were intended to achieve the highest level of action and erotic fascination.
