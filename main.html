  • ↧ The Value

    • In the Institute of Social History in Amsterdam (IISG) there exist one collection that contains pornographic elements: The complete Bill Levy archive: 'SUCK'. In this collection you'll find documents on S.E.L.F-the sexual liberation movement, SUCK-the pornographic magazine they created and the Wet Dream Film Festival-an erotic film festival they helped organised. A such in this archive you can find pornographic films that were shown during the festival.

      We live in a hypersexualised society where access to porn is at our fingertip and Internet is filled with cheaply made and quickly consumed pornographic content. The abundance of explicit content has disensitise us but when is stumbled upon a pornographic film in the Archive it came as a surprise and gave rise to this body work that deals with the value of pornography in our society. I looked at the Wet Dream Film Festival and most specifically Colt und Köcher, a movie played at the festival, to question our relation to porn.

      In constrast to the pornoggraphy we consume on the internet, the pornography in the archive can only be viewed by request and with the right equipments that are not always available. Here a movie that has been made to be consumed for arousal purposes is being locked in a fridge. With this website my aim is to give the movie it's initial purpose and to redistribute it to the public along side the dialogue and criticism it tried to explore. This website serves as an Archive and map for The Wet Dream Film Festival of 1970.

      "With the rise of the digital age, pornography has proliferated in production and public dialogue. In turn, archives and academia alike are being forced to (re)consider the value of incorporating taboo and controversial subjects and objects into their scopes and spaces.   • ↧ The Archive

    • The complete Bill Levy archive: 'SUCK'

      The collection contains documents on the sexual liberation movement centered around Suck magazine. It consist of the eight original numbers of the magazine but also documents for the Suck special issue “The Virgin Sperm Dancer”, both the Wet Dream Film Festivals, and the Wet Dreams Book. The Documents include correspondence; financial and organizational documents; articles, drawings and photographs received for publication; plans for lay-out; tapes and transcriptions of interviews; copies of some of the films shown at the Festivals. The collection also contains a collection of (both pornographic and non-pornographic) photographs with more than 1500 images and a smaller collection of unpublished material, including some unfulfilled plans for alternative projects. [2]

  • ↧ The Festival

    • The Wet Dream Film Festival is a festival that took place in Amsterdam in 1970. The Festival explores the boundaries between art and porn, voyeurism and participation, exploitation and liberation.

      It took place from the 26th till the 29th of November 1970. In venues across Amsterdam erotic films were screened, some of which were obviously meant to rise above the average porn flick and be something else. On the one hand, artistically ambitious films were screened and awarded by a jury; On the other hand screenings sometimes ended in full-blown orgies, altogether in line with the avant-garde’s ambition to replace ‘passive’ watching by active audience participation. [3]

Sexual Egalitarian & Libertarian Fraternity S.E.L.F


Tenderness, mutual respect, freedom and tolerance--these are all words we should associate with sex and love. The act of making love can be a statement of profound tenderness and concern for another human being. In this world of different languages, religions, races, cultures and classes sex is an in-commonality-something common to all. When we are unafraid and free from possessiveness it will make little difference what kind of social organisation we choose to live under, because we will be open, kind and generous. It is sexual frustration, sexual envy, sexual fear which permeates all our human relationships and which perverts them. The sexually liberated, the sexually tolerant and the sexually generous individuals are open tolerant, and generous in all their activities. Therefore S. E. L. F. wishes to encourage sexual freedom, sexual tolerance and sexual generosity.


In 1970, the Sexual Egalitarian and Libertarian Fraternity (SELF) sponsored a «Wet Dream Festival" of erotic films in Amsterdam that year:


"Our goal is sexual liberation. Our tactic the defiance of censorship. Thus expressed our aims are political, for the patterns of sexual interrelationship are created by and in tum support the other social structures. The approved sexual relationship in all western societies is exclusive, possessive, colonizing, exploitatory, sex is recognized as intimately connected with violence, for the power of the one over the other must be enforced and enforceable."


Membership of S.E.L.F included Jim Haynes, William Levy, Heathcote Williams, Germaine Greer, and Jean Shrimpton.
